RULES TO BE FOLLOWED

  • Registration for all the events is from 8:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m.
  • The registration fee for each participant is Rs. 250/- inclusive of all the events and is non-refundable.
  • Each participant must carry his/ her college ID at all times.
  • Participants must be present for the inaugural program at 8:30 a.m.
  • Teams that are late or miss their slot for any event will be disqualified.
  • Partaking in any kind of obscenity will lead to the debarring of the team from any further participation.
  • Smoking and consumption of any alcoholic beverages are strictly prohibited.
  • Participants must strictly follow the code of conduct and maintain discipline.
  • Participants must carry the required equipment for their events.
  • The organizers reserve the right to cancel, reschedule the time or change the venue of any event.
  • Decision of the judges and coordinators will be final.
  • No religious acts will be permitted.
  • Tracks for solo dance, group dance solo instrumental must be submitted in a pen drive at the time of registration.
  • The management will not be responsible for any loss of personal belongings.
  • Lunch will be provided for all the registered participants.
